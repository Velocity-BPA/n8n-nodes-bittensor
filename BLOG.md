# Automate Your Bittensor Operations with Our New n8n Community Node

We're excited to announce the release of n8n-nodes-bittensor, a powerful new community node that brings Bittensor blockchain automation to the n8n platform!

## The Challenge

Managing Bittensor operations—whether you're staking TAO, delegating to validators, monitoring subnet performance, or running validator operations—typically requires manual intervention, custom scripts, or complex technical setup. For teams managing multiple validators or tracking subnet metrics across different networks, this becomes a time-consuming bottleneck that pulls focus from strategic decision-making.

## Introducing n8n-nodes-bittensor

Velocity BPA has developed this community node to bridge the gap between Bittensor's decentralized AI network and n8n's powerful workflow automation capabilities. Now you can automate your entire Bittensor operational workflow without writing a single line of code.

## Key Features

**TAO Staking Automation**: Programmatically manage your TAO staking operations, set up automated staking strategies, and optimize your positions based on custom triggers.

**Delegation Management**: Automate delegation decisions based on validator performance metrics, automatically redistribute stakes, and maintain optimal delegation strategies.

**Subnet Queries**: Pull real-time subnet data, monitor network health, track validator performance, and gather intelligence for informed decision-making.

**Validator Operations**: Streamline validator management tasks, automate routine operational checks, and integrate validator monitoring into your existing workflows.

## Getting Started

Installing the node is simple. Just run:


npm install n8n-nodes-bittensor


Once installed, you'll find the Bittensor node in your n8n palette, ready to integrate into your workflows. Combine it with other n8n nodes to create powerful automation scenarios—send Slack notifications when validator performance drops, update spreadsheets with staking returns, or trigger rebalancing based on market conditions.
